## Preface

This book provides a single ordered path through Quantum Mechanics A Companion Guide for readers who need more than scattered fragments and prompts. It presents the material in sequence: core ideas first, then working vocabulary, then the cases and practices that make the ideas durable.

The problem it addresses is practical. Learners now access every subject through search engines, AI systems, dashboards, and partial answers. That access supplies explanations but not judgment. This book imposes enough structure to make the shape of the subject visible.

The stance is applied and instructional. It is not encyclopedic. It supplies the language, examples, and ordering needed to continue learning with sharper questions.
