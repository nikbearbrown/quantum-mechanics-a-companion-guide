## Preface

We wrote this book for readers who want more than scattered fragments, isolated prompts, or explanations that never quite connect. Our aim is to assemble the field into a clear sequence: we begin with the core ideas, then build up the working vocabulary, and finally turn to the cases and practices that make those ideas stick.

There is a practical reason we organized things this way. Today, we meet almost every subject through search engines, AI systems, dashboards, tutorials, and partial answers. That abundance is genuinely helpful, yet it brings a new challenge with it. We can gather explanation after explanation without ever building the judgment that lets us use them. This book is designed to slow us down just enough that we can see how the subject is actually put together.

Our stance throughout is applied and instructional. We are not trying to be encyclopedic. Instead, we want to give you enough language, enough examples, and enough order that you can keep learning on your own and keep asking better questions.
